    Appendix G: Instrument Illustrations and Mayo Stand Setups¶

    How to Read These Diagrams

    Each Mayo stand is shown as a top-down view. Instruments are arranged left to right in order of use: - Left zone — Approach instruments (incision, exposure) - Center zone — Procedure instruments (dissection, manipulation, hemostasis) - Right zone — Closure instruments (needle drivers, suture, dressings)

    Numbered items correspond to the legend below each diagram. Sponges are placed at the far left; loaded needle drivers at the far right. The back table holds everything not on the Mayo — only the actively needed instruments are passed to the Mayo at each phase.
