## Part I: The Approach

2. What is the AST, and what is its purpose?
3. What year was the Association of Surgical Technologists founded?
4. Describe the three phases of the surgical experience.
5. What is the difference between the scrub role and the circulating role?
6. Define "scope of practice."
7. What is HIPAA, and how does it affect the surgical technologist?

9. How many CE credits are required every four years for CST recertification?
10. Name three ethical obligations of a CST.
